Hello!

I am Rosica Kirkova (Rossi).

Creative and client focused designer, I have immersed myself in Web Design and Web Development. I use Figma for Interface Design and Prototyping, Elementor and WordPress for website building. To grow my skills I focus on developing websites using HTML, CSS and Javascript.

I have been designing for over 15 years. It all started with designing images for brochures, architectural design, graphic design, animations and in 2021, I discovered web design.

I now offer freelance design, and I am available for opportunities that will help me advance my web development skills.

Located in the UK, I am looking after my little boy and working when he is at the nursery or when he sleeps. While away from my desk I am training at my local Crossfit box, reading about coding, or watching a film if I am not falling asleep.
